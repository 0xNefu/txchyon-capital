## Why Multi-Chain Allocation Matters

As crypto expands beyond Bitcoin and Ethereum, exposure to multiple blockchains improves diversification:

- Reduces single-chain risk  
- Captures innovation across ecosystems  
- Balances returns and volatility  

> Start with core allocation: **[Crypto Portfolio Allocation for Beginners (Simple, Low-Risk Framework)](https://txchyon.com/categories/portfolio-management/)**

---

## Steps to Multi-Chain Allocation

1. Identify chains with strong fundamentals  
2. Assign allocation percentages per chain  
3. Within each chain, allocate across tokens  
4. Track portfolio performance: **[How to Track Crypto Portfolio Performance (Metrics That Actually Matter)](https://txchyon.com/categories/portfolio-management/)**  
5. Rebalance periodically: **[How to Rebalance a Crypto Portfolio (Simple Step-by-Step Guide)](https://txchyon.com/categories/portfolio-management/)**

---

## Considerations

- Correlation between chains  
- Liquidity differences  
- Transaction fees and bridging costs  
- Governance and staking mechanisms

---

## Final Thoughts

Multi-chain allocation **future-proofs your portfolio** and complements other allocation strategies like dynamic, factor-based, or volatility-adjusted approaches.
